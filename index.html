Pomfret Fry

  • Prep Time: 20 mins

  • Cook time: 10 mins

  • Recipe Servings: 2

  • 3 Fish

  • 2 tsp Ginger

  • 3 Garlic cloves

  • 1 tbsp Oil

  • 1/2 tbsp Tumeric powder

  • 2 tbsp Rec chilli powder

  • 1 lemon(juiced)

  • Pepper

  • Salt

  1. In a blender mix together ginger, garlic, oil, turmeric, red chilli and lemon juice. Masala is ready.

  2. Make gashes in the pomfret and apply the masala. Marinate it for 15 minutes.

  3. Coat it in semolina from both sides and shallow fry till golden.

  4. Serve with warm rice and your choice to curry.
